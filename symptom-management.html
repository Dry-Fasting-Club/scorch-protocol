Symptom Management & Ongoing Care

Between fasts and therapies, managing quality of life is essential. This section tracks specific symptoms and interventions.

Symptom Triage

Are you sleeping well (7+ hours)? If NO → Healing cannot occur without sleep. Prioritize sleep hygiene and supplements (Magnesium, Glycine).
Do you experience POTS (dizziness on standing)? If YES → Increase electrolytes and compression garments during refeeds.

Common Supplements (Scorch Context)

  • Electrolytes: Essential during prep and refeed (Sodium, Potassium, Magnesium).
  • Digestive Enzymes: Often needed during refeed to help weak digestion.
  • Binders: Charcoal or Zeolite (sometimes used if "herx" reactions are strong).

Lifestyle Factors

Grounding, sunlight exposure (morning), and vagus nerve stimulation (deep breathing) are non-negotiables in the protocol.
