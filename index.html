2. T3 Therapy (The Metabolic Bridge)

The "Hibernation" Breaker.
Chronic illness is often a state of functional hypothyroidism, a biological hibernation where the body prioritizes survival over repair. T3 (Triiodothyronine) is the active thyroid hormone that dictates the speed of every cell in your body. Without sufficient T3, your cells cannot oxidize glucose efficiently, leaving them depleted and reliant on stress hormones (cortisol/adrenaline) just to function.

This therapy acts as a Metabolic Bridge. By artificially restoring high T3 levels, we force the metabolism out of its cold, stagnant state. This is critical because stem cell regeneration is energy-dependent. You cannot rebuild a new body with an old, slow metabolism. T3 therapy bridges the gap, allowing for rapid glucose oxidation and accelerated healing that would normally take years to achieve through diet alone.

3. hGH Therapy (The Regenerative Signal)

Thymus & Pituitary Restoration.
While dry fasting clears the debris, Human Growth Hormone (hGH) provides the signal to rebuild. In this protocol, hGH is used specifically to target the regeneration of the Thymus Gland—the master school of the immune system. It works synergistically with T3 to prevent muscle wasting and accelerate the "anabolic rebound" during refeeding.
